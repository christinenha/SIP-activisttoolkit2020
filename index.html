Making the process towards U.S. citizenship easy for permanent Vietnamese U.S. residents

Become a U.S. citizen!

After being a permanent resident in the U.S. for a few years, you might want to have a sense of belonging to the country and to be able to access rights that are exclusive to citizens. Once you are eligible, don’t wait to get your citizenship!

Image taken from this website.

There aren’t many sites that have Vietnamese translations available, and there are fewer that will assist Vietnamese U.S. residents or immigrants through the U.S. citizen process and other processes. Often times Vietnamese people have to struggle on their own to search for help, and it can be stressful to know where to start! This website is created to support Vietnamese people and to hopefully bring attention to other audiences about the lack of Vietnamese translated material. We need more translated resources!

Image taken from this website.

Here on this site we have an organized a list of what you must complete to become a U.S. citizen, links to all the sources you need during this process, and other valuable resources.
