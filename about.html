Mission

“Assisting you through naturalization and to citizenship”

Motivation for this site

The library I volunteer at, Tully Community Branch library, happens to offer classes that help Vietnamese residents learn English and study for their naturalization tests, so I kept that resource in mind while working on this site. I thought of creating a site to help visitors at the library start their U.S. Citizenship, since it can be hard to figure out where to begin, especially if one has little knowledge of the english language. There aren’t many websites or resources that are fully in Vietnamese, so I hope that this could be helpful.
